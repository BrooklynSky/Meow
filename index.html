Siberian Cat

Siberian cats are Russia's native forest cats and are known to have existed for a long time in the dense forests of Siberia, appearing in Russian folktales, rural lore, and artwork. It was long speculated by cat experts that the ancient breed was the distant ancestor of all modern-day long-haired breeds.
